We're A Technical Student Club.

The Open Source Programming Club, VIT Chennai is a student organization that promotes the use of open source software. We are a community of students who are passionate about open source, and we believe that it is a powerful tool for innovation and collaboration.

We are always looking for new members, so if you are interested in learning more about open source software, we encourage you to join us.
